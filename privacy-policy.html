Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 21, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to CodeStore by Nathan Lobo. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to improve your experience:

  • Voluntary Information: Name or Roll Number (if provided through our analytics modal)
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, files downloaded, and interaction with features (tracked via Google Sheets API)
  • Local Storage: Theme preferences, selected tabs, and user identifiers are stored locally in your browser
  • Cookies: We may use cookies for essential site functionality and analytics

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for:

  • Understanding user behavior and improving our content
  • Personalizing your experience (e.g., remembering your theme preference)
  • Analytics and statistics (via Google Sheets integration)
  • Displaying relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: To display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits. Learn more at Google's Ad Policy.
  • Google Apps Script: For analytics tracking via Google Sheets
  • GitHub: We host code repositories and files on GitHub

Data Storage and Security

Most data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. Analytics data is sent to our Google Sheets via Google Apps Script. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties except as described in this policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage at any time to remove stored preferences
  • Opt-out of providing your name/roll number (use the "Skip" option in our modal)
  • Use browser extensions or settings to block cookies and tracking
  • Contact us to request deletion of any analytics data we've collected

Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for educational purposes and may be used by students. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent. If you believe we have collected information from a child inappropriately, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
